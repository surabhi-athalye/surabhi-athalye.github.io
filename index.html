(Website under construction) I am a PhD student at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Georgia Tech, where I work with Prof Kyriakos G. Vamvoudakis.

Research Interests

My research focuses on the control and security of cyber-physical systems, integrating concepts from control theory, game theory, optimization and machine learning. I develop model-based and model-free (data-driven) approaches for solving optimal control problems.
